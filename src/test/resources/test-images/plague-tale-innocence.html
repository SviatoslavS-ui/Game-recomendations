A Plague Tale: Innocence

Follow the grim tale of young Amicia and her little brother Hugo in a heartrending journey through the darkest hours of history. Hunted by Inquisition soldiers and surrounded by unstoppable swarms of rats, they will struggle to survive against overwhelming odds and fight to find purpose in this brutal, unforgiving world.

Set in 14th century France during the time of the Black Death, this emotional adventure combines stealth, puzzle-solving, and resource management as you guide these siblings through a nightmarish vision of history, where innocence is confronted by the harsh realities of a world consumed by plague and war.

Key Features

  • Guide Amicia and Hugo through plague-ravaged France.
  • Use stealth to evade the relentless Inquisition.
  • Solve environmental puzzles with light and fire.
  • Craft alchemical items to outwit your enemies.
  • Master the art of distraction and misdirection to outsmart your enemies in a world where you're always outnumbered.
  • Navigate through stunning medieval landscapes.
  • Experience a touching sibling survival story.
  • Survive against overwhelming swarms of rats.
  • Shape your path through meaningful choices.
  • Experience a hauntingly beautiful soundtrack that perfectly captures the game's emotional depth and atmosphere.
