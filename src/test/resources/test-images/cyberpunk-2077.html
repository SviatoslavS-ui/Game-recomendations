Cyberpunk 2077

In the dystopian metropolis of Night City, you play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality.

This open-world action-adventure RPG from CD Projekt Red takes place in a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification. Customize your character's cyberware, skillset, and playstyle as you explore a vast city where your choices shape the story and the world around you.

Key Features

  • Immersive first-person perspective in a massive open world
  • Three distinct lifepaths affecting story and dialogue
  • Extensive character customization and cybernetic augmentations
  • Fluid combat system combining gunplay, melee, and hacking
  • Branching narrative with multiple endings based on player choices
  • Complex hacking mechanics to manipulate the environment and enemies
  • Diverse skill trees allowing for various playstyles
  • Detailed crafting and upgrade systems for weapons and gear
  • Vehicle customization and high-speed chases throughout Night City
  • Deep relationship systems with romance options and companion storylines
