Cyberpunk 2077

In the dystopian metropolis of Night City, you play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality.

This open-world action-adventure RPG from CD Projekt Red takes place in a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification. Customize your character's cyberware, skillset, and playstyle as you explore a vast city where your choices shape the story and the world around you.

Key Features

  • Immersive first-person Night City exploration
  • Three unique lifepaths shape your character's story
  • Extensive character customization with cybernetic augments that enhance combat abilities and tactics
  • Multi-style combat system with guns and hacking
  • Branching narrative paths lead to multiple endings.
  • Hack, control, or sabotage Night City's systems.
  • Versatile skill trees for unique playstyles
  • Deep weapon and gear crafting and upgrade system
  • Deep character relationships evolving through dialogue choices and romance options in Night City's underworld
  • Vehicle customization and street racing events
