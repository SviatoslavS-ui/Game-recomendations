Perfect Dark

As Carrington Institute's most promising new Agent, Joanna Dark must uncover the truth behind the dataDyne Corporation's recent technological breakthroughs.

This revolutionary first-person shooter from Rare pushed the Nintendo 64 to its limits with advanced AI, sophisticated weapon systems, and a compelling sci-fi narrative. Set in a near-future world of corporate espionage and alien conspiracy.

Key Features

  • Battle intelligent enemies, coordinate with allies
  • Master advanced weapons with dual firing functions
  • Experience revolutionary multiplayer with advanced AI and customizable scenarios
  • Navigate complex security systems with stealth
  • Uncover alien conspiracies in thrilling storyline
  • Wield powerful arsenal of futuristic weapons
  • Choose difficulty modes affecting mission goals
  • Challenge friends in counter-operative mode
  • Immerse yourself in a groundbreaking espionage adventure with lifelike characters
  • Experience dynamic soundscapes and ambient effects
