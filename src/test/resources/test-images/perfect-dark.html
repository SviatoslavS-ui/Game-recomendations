Perfect Dark

As Carrington Institute's most promising new Agent, Joanna Dark must uncover the truth behind the dataDyne Corporation's recent technological breakthroughs.

This revolutionary first-person shooter from Rare pushed the Nintendo 64 to its limits with advanced AI, sophisticated weapon systems, and a compelling sci-fi narrative. Set in a near-future world of corporate espionage and alien conspiracy.

Key Features

  • Advanced AI with intelligent enemy behavior and teammate assistance
  • Dual-function weapons with primary and secondary firing modes
  • Sophisticated multiplayer with bots and customizable scenarios
  • Stealth gameplay elements with security systems and alarms
  • Compelling sci-fi storyline involving alien technology
  • Extensive weapon arsenal including futuristic firearms
  • Multiple difficulty levels affecting story elements
  • Counter-operative mode allowing second player interference
  • Detailed character animations and facial expressions
  • Immersive sound design with dynamic music system
