Disco Elysium

A groundbreaking open world RPG where you play as a detective with a unique skill system. Interrogate characters, solve murders, or embrace corruption in this narrative-driven experience.

This revolutionary RPG focuses entirely on character interaction and dialogue. Play as a troubled detective investigating a murder while battling your own inner demons in the decaying city of Martinaise.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary dialogue system replaces combat arts
  • Unique skills shape your detective's personality
  • Deep ideological system explores social discourse
  • Develop your character through an innovative system of thoughts and memories
  • Solve complex mysteries through multiple approaches
  • Experience rich world filled with social themes
  • Discover vast narrative with exceptional dialogue
  • Shape your detective through meaningful choices
  • Explore a unique painterly world where every visual element tells a story of urban decay
  • Engage with inner voices that guide investigations
