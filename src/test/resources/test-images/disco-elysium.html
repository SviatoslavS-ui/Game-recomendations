Disco Elysium

A groundbreaking open world RPG where you play as a detective with a unique skill system. Interrogate characters, solve murders, or embrace corruption in this narrative-driven experience.

This revolutionary RPG focuses entirely on character interaction and dialogue. Play as a troubled detective investigating a murder while battling your own inner demons in the decaying city of Martinaise.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary dialogue-based RPG with no traditional combat
  • 24 unique skills that act as internal voices and personalities
  • Deep political and philosophical themes
  • Thought Cabinet system for developing ideologies and beliefs
  • Extensive character customization
  • Multiple approaches to solving the central murder mystery
  • Rich world-building with detailed political and social commentary
  • Exceptional writing with millions of words of dialogue
  • Unique art style with painterly visuals
  • Choices that fundamentally shape your character's identity
