Dead Space

Set in the year 2508, Dead Space is a groundbreaking sci-fi survival horror game that follows engineer Isaac Clarke aboard the USG Ishimura, a massive deep-space mining ship that's gone dark. What starts as a routine repair mission becomes a nightmare as Isaac discovers the ship's crew has been slaughtered and infected by an alien scourge.

As the lone survivor, Isaac must navigate through the darkness of the Ishimura, fighting grotesque monsters called Necromorphs while uncovering a conspiracy involving the mysterious Markers and dealing with his own deteriorating sanity as he searches for his girlfriend Nicole.

Key Features

  • Dismember enemies strategically to defeat them
  • Zero-G sections feature intricate environmental puzzles and intense combat scenarios in weightless space
  • Holographic HUD integrated into Isaac's suit
  • Use Kinesis and Stasis powers to solve puzzles
  • Customize and upgrade weapons at workbenches
  • Navigate vast vacuum zones with oxygen management
  • Enemies adapt their tactics based on damage
  • Strategic resource and inventory management
  • Unlock advanced content and challenge in Game Plus
  • Experience terrifying psychological manifestations while uncovering the dark truth of the Ishimura
