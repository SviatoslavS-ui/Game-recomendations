Red Dead Redemption 2

An epic tale of life in America's unforgiving heartland. After a robbery goes badly wrong, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee.

Set in 1899 America, this prequel to Red Dead Redemption delivers an unprecedented level of detail in its open-world Western setting. Players experience the decline of the outlaw era through the eyes of Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang.

Key Features

  • Massive open-world recreation of 1899 America
  • Deep honor system affecting story and character interactions
  • Realistic horse bonding and care mechanics
  • Hunting and fishing with detailed animal behavior
  • Gang camp management with upgrades and relationships
  • Detailed weapon maintenance and customization
  • Dynamic weather and day/night cycle affecting gameplay
  • Red Dead Online multiplayer with persistent world
  • Photography mode for capturing scenic moments
  • Immersive activities like poker, dominoes, and five finger fillet
