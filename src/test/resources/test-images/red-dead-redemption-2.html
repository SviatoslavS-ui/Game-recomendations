Red Dead Redemption 2

An epic tale of life in America's unforgiving heartland. After a robbery goes badly wrong, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee.

Set in 1899 America, this prequel to Red Dead Redemption delivers an unprecedented level of detail in its open-world Western setting. Players experience the decline of the outlaw era through the eyes of Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang.

Key Features

  • Explore stunning landscapes of 1899 West America
  • Shape your path through an immersive honor system
  • Experience an authentic Wild West adventure with deep character relationships and choices
  • Master horse riding through bonding and training
  • Hunt wildlife using detailed tracking mechanics
  • Manage your gang's camp with crucial upgrades
  • Customize and maintain period-accurate weapons
  • Adapt to dynamic weather affecting survival
  • Discover a living breathing world where every activity shapes your frontier experience
  • Capture breathtaking moments in photography mode
