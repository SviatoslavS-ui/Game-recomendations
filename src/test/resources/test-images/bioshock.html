BioShock

After your plane crashes into icy uncharted waters, you discover Rapture, a city hidden beneath the sea with a dark secret.

Explore the underwater dystopia of Rapture, a failed utopian society where genetic modification has run amok. This immersive first-person shooter blends action with deep philosophical themes and moral choices.

Key Features

  • Explore mysterious underwater metropolis Rapture
  • Enhance your abilities with genetic modifications
  • Shape the story through impactful moral decisions
  • Haunting symbiosis of innocent waifs Little Sisters and metal titans Big Daddies
  • Experience unique Art Deco design in sunken city
  • Weapons upgrade and customization with various arsenal evolution and ingenious ammunition types
  • Uncover dark secrets through scattered audio logs
  • Master intricate puzzles to breach security systems
  • Discover deep themes of human nature and free will
  • Explore atmospheric ruins of underwater utopia
