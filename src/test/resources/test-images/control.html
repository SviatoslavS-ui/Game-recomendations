Control

After searching for answers about her supernatural past, Jesse Faden finds herself in the Federal Bureau of Control, a secretive agency in New York. What begins as a search for her missing brother becomes a battle for survival against a mysterious otherworldly threat.

This supernatural action-adventure game from Remedy Entertainment combines third-person gunplay with paranormal abilities in a constantly shifting brutalist architecture. As the new Director of the Bureau, you must master supernatural powers, modifiable weapons, and reactive environments to defeat a deadly enemy known only as the Hiss.

Key Features

  • Dynamic supernatural abilities including telekinesis and levitation
  • Transforming Service Weapon with multiple forms
  • Metroidvania-inspired world design with interconnected sectors and premises
  • Reality-bending environments that shift and transform
  • Rich narrative with connections to Alan Wake
  • Challenging boss encounters with unique mechanics
  • Extensive lore through collectible documents and recordings
  • Ability and weapon modification system with craftable upgrades
  • Distinctive brutalist architecture and surreal immersive visual design
  • Reactive destruction system affecting combat environments
