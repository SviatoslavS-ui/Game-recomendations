Wolfenstein II: The New Colossus

America, 1961. The Nazis have taken over the world, and established a new order. BJ Blazkowicz, war hero and last hope of the resistance, must rally the underground fighters to spark the second American Revolution.

This action-packed first-person shooter continues the story of William "BJ" Blazkowicz in an alternate history where the Nazis won World War II. Armed with an arsenal of powerful weapons and newfound abilities, you'll battle Nazi war machines and supersoldiers across an occupied America as you fight to free the world from tyranny.

Key Features

  • Dual-wielding weapon system with unique weapon combinations
  • Intense first-person combat with visceral takedown mechanics
  • Alternate history narrative with cinematic storytelling
  • Diverse environments across Nazi-occupied America
  • Upgradable weapons with specialized modifications
  • Stealth mechanics allowing for multiple approach
  • Unique contraptions enhancing movement and combat
  • Challenging commander system with tactical enemy encounters
  • Rich character development with emotional story arcs
  • Detailed retro-futuristic Nazi technology and architecture
