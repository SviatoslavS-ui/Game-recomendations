The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Play any type of character you can imagine in this open-world fantasy epic set in a vast world filled with dragons, ancient ruins, and limitless adventure.

As the Dragonborn, master the ancient power of the Voice and face the return of dragons to Skyrim. This legendary RPG offers unprecedented freedom to explore, create, and shape your own destiny in the frozen northern province.

Key Features

  • Explore vast untamed wilderness of mythical Skyrim
  • Master ancient Dragon Shouts with devastating power
  • Experience an epic journey through a stunning realm of dragons and ancient Nordic ruins
  • Create unique heroes through skill-based growth
  • Battle fierce dragons in spectacular aerial combat
  • Join legendary guilds in treacherous quests
  • Forge mighty weapons with mystical enchantments
  • Build your legacy through property and marriage
  • Discover an endless adventure with dynamic quests and countless dungeons to explore
  • Customize your adventure with powerful modding tools
