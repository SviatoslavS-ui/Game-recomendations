The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Play any type of character you can imagine in this open-world fantasy epic set in a vast world filled with dragons, ancient ruins, and limitless adventure.

As the Dragonborn, master the ancient power of the Voice and face the return of dragons to Skyrim. This legendary RPG offers unprecedented freedom to explore, create, and shape your own destiny in the frozen northern province.

Key Features

  • Massive open world with complete freedom of exploration
  • Dragon Shouts system with powerful Voice abilities
  • Classless character progression with skill-based leveling
  • Extensive modding support with Creation Kit tools
  • Dragon encounters with dynamic aerial combat
  • Multiple guild questlines including Thieves Guild and Dark Brotherhood
  • Crafting systems for weapons, armor, and enchantments
  • Hundreds of dungeons, caves, and ancient ruins to explore
  • Marriage system and home ownership options
  • Radiant quest system providing endless content
