Super Mario Galaxy

The ultimate Nintendo hero is taking the ultimate step... out into space. Join Mario as he ushers in a new era of video games, defying gravity across all the planets in the galaxy.

Set in outer space, Mario travels through different galaxies collecting Power Stars. Each galaxy contains unique planetoids with their own gravitational forces, allowing players to walk sideways or upside down in this revolutionary 3D platforming experience.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary gravity-based gameplay
  • 42 unique galaxies with diverse astronomical environments
  • Innovative Wii Remote motion controls
  • New "Spin" technique for combat and puzzle-solving
  • Star Bits collection and feeding system with Hungry Lumas
  • Pull Stars for space traversal and Launch Stars for long distances
  • Mix of 3D exploration and 2D platforming sections
  • Comet Observatory hub world
  • Unlockable Luigi gameplay after collecting 120 Power Stars
  • Special motion-controlled levels
