The Orange Box

Games included: Half-Life 2, Half-Life 2: Episode One, Half-Life 2: Episode Two, Portal, and Team Fortress 2.

This legendary compilation package from Valve includes five groundbreaking games that defined modern PC gaming. From the physics-based gameplay of Half-Life 2 to the innovative puzzle mechanics of Portal.

Key Features

  • Five complete games in one comprehensive package
  • Half-Life 2 with revolutionary physics-based gameplay
  • Portal's innovative puzzle mechanics with portal gun
  • Team Fortress 2's class-based multiplayer combat
  • Half-Life 2 episodes continuing Gordon Freeman's story
  • Source engine showcasing advanced graphics and physics
  • Steam integration for seamless multiplayer experience
  • Hundreds of hours of single and multiplayer content
  • Achievement system across all included games
  • Exceptional value with multiple award-winning titles
