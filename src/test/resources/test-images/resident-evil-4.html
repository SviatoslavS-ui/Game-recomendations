Resident Evil 4

Leon S. Kennedy is on a top secret mission to investigate the disappearance of the president's daughter in a mysterious location in Europe.

This revolutionary survival horror game reinvented the Resident Evil series with over-the-shoulder camera perspective and action-oriented gameplay. Experience intense combat against the Las Plagas-infected villagers in rural Spain.
