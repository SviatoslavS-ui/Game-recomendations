Half-Life 2

By taking the suspense, challenge and visceral charge of the original, Half-Life 2 opens the door to a world where the player's presence affects everything around him.

Gordon Freeman returns to face the alien Combine occupation of Earth. This groundbreaking sequel features revolutionary physics gameplay, emotional storytelling, and the iconic Gravity Gun that transforms the environment into your weapon.

Key Features

  • Experience revolutionary physics with Source engine
  • Weaponize your environment with the Gravity Gun
  • Immerse yourself in a seamless story where every moment drives the narrative forward
  • Journey through City 17 and coastal highways
  • Interact with lifelike AI-driven characters
  • Master thrilling vehicle segments across terrain
  • Participate in innovative physics-based puzzles
  • Meet memorable characters shaping your journey
  • Experience groundbreaking gameplay combining physics puzzles with intense combat
  • Discover Earth's dark future under alien control
