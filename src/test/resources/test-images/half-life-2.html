Half-Life 2

By taking the suspense, challenge and visceral charge of the original, Half-Life 2 opens the door to a world where the player's presence affects everything around him.

Gordon Freeman returns to face the alien Combine occupation of Earth. This groundbreaking sequel features revolutionary physics gameplay, emotional storytelling, and the iconic Gravity Gun that transforms the environment into your weapon.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary Source engine with realistic physics
  • Iconic Gravity Gun for manipulating objects as weapons
  • Immersive storytelling without cutscenes
  • Diverse environments from City 17 to coastal highways
  • Advanced AI with realistic character interactions
  • Vehicle sections including airboat and buggy sequences
  • Environmental puzzles utilizing physics mechanics
  • Memorable characters like Alyx Vance and Dr. Kleiner
  • Seamless integration of combat and puzzle-solving
  • Atmospheric world-building in dystopian Earth setting
