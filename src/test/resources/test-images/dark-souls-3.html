Dark Souls III

As fire fades and the world falls into ruin, journey into a universe filled with more colossal enemies and environments. Players will be immersed into a world of epic atmosphere and darkness through faster gameplay and amplified combat intensity.

This action RPG continues the critically acclaimed Dark Souls series with enhanced combat mechanics and an expanded world. As the Champion of Ash, you must return the Lords of Cinder to their thrones in Lothric to link the fire once more, or usher in the Age of Dark by letting the flames fade.

  • Master combat system with weapon arts and skills
  • Explore vast interconnected world with atmospheric locations and hidden paths in dark realms
  • Create unique characters with many equipment sets
  • Overcome challenging bosses with multiple phases
  • Join multiplayer battles through dedicated servers
