Dark Souls III

As fire fades and the world falls into ruin, journey into a universe filled with more colossal enemies and environments. Players will be immersed into a world of epic atmosphere and darkness through faster gameplay and amplified combat intensity.

This action RPG continues the critically acclaimed Dark Souls series with enhanced combat mechanics and an expanded world. As the Champion of Ash, you must return the Lords of Cinder to their thrones in Lothric to link the fire once more, or usher in the Age of Dark by letting the flames fade.

Key Features

  • Refined combat system with weapon arts and battle skills
  • Interconnected world with diverse, rich atmospheric environments
  • Expanded character customization and equipment options
  • Challenging boss fights with multi-phase transformations
  • Enhanced online multiplayer with dedicated servers
  • Covenant system with unique rewards and objectives
  • Expanded magic system with FP management
  • Dynamic lighting and particle effects enhancing immersion
  • Lore-rich storytelling through environmental details
  • Expanded New Game Plus with additional challenges
