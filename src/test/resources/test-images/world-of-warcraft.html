World of Warcraft

Enter Azeroth, a vast world of myth, magic, and endless adventure. Choose your faction—Alliance or Horde—and embark on an epic journey through a land beset by conflict and shaped by thousands of years of warfare, intrigue, and heroism.

As the world's most popular MMORPG, World of Warcraft offers limitless possibilities for players to customize their experience. Create and develop your character, form powerful alliances, conquer challenging dungeons, and leave your mark on a rich fantasy universe that continues to evolve with regular content updates.

Key Features

  • Massive, legendary open world spanning multiple continents and fantasy realms
  • Character customization based on races and classes
  • PvE content including dungeons, raids, and quests
  • PvP gameplay with battlegrounds, arenas, world PvP
  • Rich lore spanning decades of storytelling
  • Social features including guilds and communities
  • Regular expansion packs adding new content, zones, heroes and features
  • Professions and economy systems, crafting and trade
  • Achievement system with thousands of challenges
  • Seasonal events and limited-time content
