World of Warcraft

Enter Azeroth, a vast world of myth, magic, and endless adventure. Choose your faction—Alliance or Horde—and embark on an epic journey through a land beset by conflict and shaped by thousands of years of warfare, intrigue, and heroism.

As the world's most popular MMORPG, World of Warcraft offers limitless possibilities for players to customize their experience. Create and develop your character, form powerful alliances, conquer challenging dungeons, and leave your mark on a rich fantasy universe that continues to evolve with regular content updates.

Key Features

  • Explore famous Azeroth, a world of myth, magic, and endless adventure for you and your friends
  • Create your own legendary hero, choose class and race
  • Embark on an epic immersive journey through mystical realms filled with ancient power
  • Battle fierce monsters in challenging dungeons
  • Compete in thrilling arenas and epic battlegrounds
  • Join powerful guilds to forge lasting alliances
  • Master ancient professions to forge legendary gear
  • Discover treasures in dangerous monster lairs
  • Experience an epic saga where mighty heroes shape the destiny of countless realms
  • Conquer challenges to earn legendary rewards
