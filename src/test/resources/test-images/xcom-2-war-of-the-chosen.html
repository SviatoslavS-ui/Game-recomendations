XCOM 2: War of the Chosen

Earth has fallen to an alien occupation. As the leader of a guerrilla force facing impossible odds, you must ignite a global resistance to eliminate the alien threat.

This expansion to the award-winning XCOM 2 adds new content and challenges to the campaign as you face off against "The Chosen," three powerful alien champions pursuing the Commander. Recruit three new resistance factions to your cause as you deploy new soldier classes, abilities, and strategies to counter the enemy threat.

Key Features

  • Face three deadly Chosen with devastating powers
  • Command tactical squads in destructible battlefields
  • Lead powerful resistance factions wielding extraordinary abilities and advanced tech
  • Direct global operations from mobile command base
  • Forge unbreakable bonds between elite soldiers
  • Execute strategic covert infiltration missions
  • Deploy advanced weapons and specialized tactics
  • Battle through hordes of mutated creatures
  • Experience intense tactical warfare where every choice shapes humanity's destiny
  • Create inspiring propaganda to boost resistance
