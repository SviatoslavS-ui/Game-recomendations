XCOM 2: War of the Chosen

Earth has fallen to an alien occupation. As the leader of a guerrilla force facing impossible odds, you must ignite a global resistance to eliminate the alien threat.

This expansion to the award-winning XCOM 2 adds new content and challenges to the campaign as you face off against "The Chosen," three powerful alien champions pursuing the Commander. Recruit three new resistance factions to your cause as you deploy new soldier classes, abilities, and strategies to counter the enemy threat.

Key Features

  • Three unique enemy Chosen with distinct abilities
  • Turn-based tactical combat with destructible environments
  • Three new resistance factions with specialized soldier classes and unique abilities
  • Advanced strategy layer with global management
  • Bond system allowing soldiers to develop relationships
  • New mission types including covert operations
  • Expanded arsenal of weapons, armor, and equipment
  • The Lost - mutated humans that attack both XCOM and aliens
  • Photobooth system for creating custom propaganda
  • Challenge Mode with weekly custom scenarios
