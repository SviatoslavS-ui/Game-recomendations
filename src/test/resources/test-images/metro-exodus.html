Metro Exodus

Embark on an epic journey across post-apocalyptic Russia in this story-driven first-person shooter. As Artyom, you must lead a band of Spartan Rangers on a continent-spanning journey to find a new home beyond the shattered ruins of Moscow.

Metro Exodus blends deadly combat with stealth, exploration, and survival horror in one of gaming's most immersive worlds. Experience dynamic weather, day-night cycles, and environments that change with the seasons as you face terrifying mutants and hostile human factions in this atmospheric survival epic.

Key Features

  • Explore vast post-apocalyptic Russian landscapes
  • Experience dynamic weather across day and night
  • Master intricate survival systems with crafting and resource management mechanics
  • Customize weapons with tactical modifications
  • Shape the fate of companions through decisions
  • Choose between stealth and aggressive tactics
  • Face terrifying mutants in atmospheric combat
  • Adapt your strategy to seasonal challenges
  • Experience a rich narrative where your choices shape the fate of your companions
  • Witness stunning visuals with next-gen ray tracing
