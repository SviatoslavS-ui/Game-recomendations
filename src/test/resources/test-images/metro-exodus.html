Metro Exodus

Embark on an epic journey across post-apocalyptic Russia in this story-driven first-person shooter. As Artyom, you must lead a band of Spartan Rangers on a continent-spanning journey to find a new home beyond the shattered ruins of Moscow.

Metro Exodus blends deadly combat with stealth, exploration, and survival horror in one of gaming's most immersive worlds. Experience dynamic weather, day-night cycles, and environments that change with the seasons as you face terrifying mutants and hostile human factions in this atmospheric survival epic.

Key Features

  • Vast open environments with non-linear level design
  • Dynamic weather system and day-night cycle
  • Immersive survival mechanics including crafting, ammunition and resource management
  • Customizable weapons with extensive modification
  • Reactive realistic world where player choices affect companion survival
  • Stealth and combat approaches with moral consequences
  • Atmospheric horror elements and tense encounters
  • Seasonal changes affecting gameplay and world
  • Rich storytelling with memorable characters, complex relationships and multiple endings
  • Stunning visuals with advanced lighting and physics
