DOOM Eternal

The direct sequel to DOOM (2016) that delivers the ultimate combination of speed, power, and aggressive first-person combat as you battle demons across dimensions.

Hell's armies have invaded Earth. As the Doom Slayer, you must rip and tear through demonic hordes using an expanded arsenal of weapons and abilities in this relentlessly fast-paced shooter.

Key Features

  • Relentlessly fast-paced demon-slaying action
  • Enhanced mobility with dash, climbing, and monkey bars
  • Expanded weapon arsenal with upgrades
  • Resource management through glory kills and chainsaw fuel
  • Flame Belch and Ice Bomb equipment
  • Battlemode asymmetric multiplayer
  • Challenging platforming sections
  • Heavy metal soundtrack that perfectly fits gameplay
  • Stunning visuals showcasing hellish and futuristic environments
  • Master Levels providing extreme combat challenges
