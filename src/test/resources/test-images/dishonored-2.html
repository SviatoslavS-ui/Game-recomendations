Dishonored 2

Reprise your role as a supernatural assassin in Dishonored 2. Play as either Emily Kaldwin or Corvo Attano in a world where mysticism and industry collide. Will you choose to play as Empress Emily Kaldwin or as the royal protector, Corvo Attano?

Fifteen years after the events of the first game, an otherworldly usurper has seized the throne from Empress Emily Kaldwin. Explore a new setting in the coastal city of Karnaca, hunt down your enemies, and forever alter the fate of the Empire with your choices.

Key Features

  • Choose between Emily or Corvo with unique powers
  • Master stealth mechanics with vertical traversal
  • Choose between lethal and non-lethal approaches with consequences that shape the Empire
  • Navigate complex levels with multiple pathways
  • Combine supernatural powers in creative ways
  • Experience missions with time-bending powers
  • Face adaptive AI that responds to your tactics
  • Explore stunning world powered by Void Engine
  • Make crucial choices that affect the Empire through branching story paths and outcomes
  • Discover rich stories within Karnaca's streets
