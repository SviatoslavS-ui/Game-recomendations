Dishonored 2

Reprise your role as a supernatural assassin in Dishonored 2. Play as either Emily Kaldwin or Corvo Attano in a world where mysticism and industry collide. Will you choose to play as Empress Emily Kaldwin or as the royal protector, Corvo Attano?

Fifteen years after the events of the first game, an otherworldly usurper has seized the throne from Empress Emily Kaldwin. Explore a new setting in the coastal city of Karnaca, hunt down your enemies, and forever alter the fate of the Empire with your choices.

Key Features

  • Choose between two characters with unique abilities
  • Enhanced stealth system with vertical traversal
  • Non-lethal and lethal approaches with moral consequences related to your choices
  • Intricate level design with multiple paths
  • Expanded supernatural powers with creative combinations and unique gadgets
  • Innovative mission design including time manipulation and possession
  • Responsive AI that adapts to your playstyle
  • Stunning visual design with improved Void Engine
  • Branching interactive narrative with multiple endings based on choices
  • Rich environmental storytelling in city of Karnaca
