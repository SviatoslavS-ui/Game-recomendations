SoulCalibur

This is a tale of souls and swords, transcending the world and all its history, told for all eternity. The greatest weapons-based fighter returns.

SoulCalibur revolutionized 3D fighting games with its innovative eight-way run system and weapon-based combat. Each character wields a unique weapon with distinct fighting styles, creating a deep and strategic combat experience.

Key Features

  • Master innovative eight-directional combat system
  • Wield unique weapons with distinctive fighting arts
  • Experience the pinnacle of weapon-based combat with innovative fighting mechanics
  • Execute powerful combos through buffer mechanics
  • Master defensive Guard Impact counter techniques
  • Choose from legendary warriors with unique arms
  • Deploy strategic combat with advanced tactics
  • Witness breathtaking battles and animations
  • Discover an extensive roster of warriors each mastering their own legendary weapons
  • Challenge opponents in various combat arenas
