Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Fortune hunter Nathan Drake returns in a dangerous game of cat-and-mouse against a fugitive war criminal in search of the legendary Himalayan valley of Shambhala.

This cinematic action-adventure masterpiece combines platforming, puzzle-solving, and third-person shooting in exotic locations around the world. Experience movie-quality storytelling with incredible set pieces and character development.

Key Features

  • Cinematic storytelling with movie-quality production values
  • Seamless blend of platforming, shooting, and puzzle-solving
  • Spectacular set pieces including train sequences
  • Exotic locations from Nepal to the Himalayas
  • Advanced climbing and traversal mechanics
  • Cover-based third-person shooting system
  • Compelling characters with excellent voice acting
  • Treasure hunting with hidden collectibles
  • Competitive and cooperative multiplayer modes
  • Stunning visuals pushing PlayStation 3 hardware limits
