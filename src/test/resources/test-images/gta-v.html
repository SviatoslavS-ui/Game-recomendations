Grand Theft Auto V

The sprawling sun-soaked metropolis of Los Santos is chock full of self-help coaches, starlets and C-List celebrities struggling to stay relevant.

Experience the intertwining stories of three unique protagonists in the largest and most ambitious Grand Theft Auto yet. Los Santos and Blaine County offer unprecedented variety in landscapes, from urban sprawl to desert wilderness.

Key Features

  • Three playable protagonists with unique abilities and storylines
  • Massive open world spanning Los Santos and Blaine County
  • Dynamic character switching during missions
  • Extensive vehicle customization and modification
  • Stock market system affected by player actions
  • Heist missions requiring planning and crew selection
  • GTA Online with persistent multiplayer world
  • First-person mode for immersive gameplay
  • Advanced physics engine and realistic vehicle handling
  • Diverse activities including golf, tennis, skydiving, and racing
