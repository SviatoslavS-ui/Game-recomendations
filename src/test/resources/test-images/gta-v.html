Grand Theft Auto V

The sprawling sun-soaked metropolis of Los Santos is chock full of self-help coaches, starlets and C-List celebrities struggling to stay relevant.

Experience the intertwining stories of three unique protagonists in the largest and most ambitious Grand Theft Auto yet. Los Santos and Blaine County offer unprecedented variety in landscapes, from urban sprawl to desert wilderness.

Key Features

  • Control three unique characters with special skills
  • Explore vast landscapes of Los Santos and beyond
  • Experience seamless character switching with dynamic missions and unique storylines
  • Customize vehicles with extensive modifications
  • Manipulate markets through criminal activities
  • Plan elaborate heists with specialized crew teams
  • Experience action from first-person perspective
  • Master realistic driving with advanced physics
  • Discover a living world filled with activities from golf to skydiving missions
  • Join persistent online world with endless adventures
