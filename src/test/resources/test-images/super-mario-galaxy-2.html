Super Mario Galaxy 2

The sequel to the galaxy-hopping original game, loaded with entirely new galaxies and features to challenge players.

Building upon the revolutionary gravity-based gameplay of its predecessor, Super Mario Galaxy 2 introduces dynamic environments, new power-ups, and the beloved dinosaur companion Yoshi. With 49 galaxies across seven worlds, this sequel offers 242 unique Power Stars to collect.

Key Features

  • 49 unique galaxies with dynamic, music-synchronized environments
  • Yoshi partnership with tongue mechanics
  • Three special Yoshi fruits: Dash Pepper, Blimp Fruit, and Bulb Berry
  • Enhanced Prankster Comets with varied challenge types
  • Starship Mario hub world that expands
  • 242 total Power Stars including 120 challenging Green Stars
  • Time-manipulation switches and gravity-shifting platforms
  • Cosmic Clones that mirror player movements
  • Map-based progression system
