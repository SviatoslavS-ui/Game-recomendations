Tekken 3

An ancient evil force has reawakened. To lure it out of hiding will take the greatest fighting contest the world has ever seen.

The pinnacle of 3D fighting games, Tekken 3 introduced fluid movement, sidestepping mechanics, and a diverse roster of fighters. This arcade masterpiece set the standard for all future 3D fighting games.

Key Features

  • Master revolutionary three-dimensional combat system
  • Execute precise sidesteps for strategic advantage
  • Experience the ultimate evolution of 3D fighting with groundbreaking combat mechanics
  • Battle through challenging Tekken Force missions
  • Compete in unique Tekken Ball volleyball mode
  • Witness fluid combat with perfect animations
  • Unlock powerful fighters and special costumes
  • Master moves in comprehensive practice mode
  • Choose from an extensive roster of warriors each mastering unique martial arts
  • Feel the intensity with dynamic combat music
