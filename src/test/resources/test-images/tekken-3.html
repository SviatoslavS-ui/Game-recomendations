Tekken 3

An ancient evil force has reawakened. To lure it out of hiding will take the greatest fighting contest the world has ever seen.

The pinnacle of 3D fighting games, Tekken 3 introduced fluid movement, sidestepping mechanics, and a diverse roster of fighters. This arcade masterpiece set the standard for all future 3D fighting games.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary 3D fighting with full 360-degree movement
  • Sidestep mechanics for true three-dimensional combat
  • Diverse roster of fighters with unique martial arts styles
  • Tekken Force beat-em-up adventure mode
  • Tekken Ball volleyball mini-game
  • Smooth 60fps gameplay with fluid animations
  • Multiple unlockable characters and costumes
  • Arcade-perfect home console conversion
  • Training mode for mastering complex combos
  • Memorable soundtrack enhancing the fighting atmosphere
