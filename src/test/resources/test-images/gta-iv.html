Grand Theft Auto IV

What does the American Dream mean today? For Niko Belic, fresh off the boat from Europe, it's the hope he can escape his past.

Set in the sprawling fictional Liberty City, this action-adventure masterpiece delivers an unprecedented open-world experience. Players control Niko Bellic, an Eastern European war veteran, as he navigates the criminal underworld in pursuit of the American Dream.

Key Features

  • Massive open-world Liberty City with multiple boroughs to explore
  • Advanced cover system for tactical third-person combat
  • Six-star wanted system with dynamic police response
  • Individual body part targeting in combat
  • Morality choices that alter the storyline
  • Social activities like bowling, darts, and hanging out with friends
  • Cell phone system for communication and multiplayer access
  • In-game internet with email and dating features
  • Subway system for quick city traversal
  • 32-player online multiplayer with various game modes
