Grand Theft Auto IV

What does the American Dream mean today? For Niko Belic, fresh off the boat from Europe, it's the hope he can escape his past.

Set in the sprawling fictional Liberty City, this action-adventure masterpiece delivers an unprecedented open-world experience. Players control Niko Bellic, an Eastern European war veteran, as he navigates the criminal underworld in pursuit of the American Dream.

Key Features

  • Explore detailed districts of vast Liberty City
  • Master tactical combat with advanced cover mechanics
  • Experience a living city with dynamic social activities and meaningful relationships
  • Face escalating police response across six levels
  • Shape Niko's story through impactful decisions
  • Engage enemies with precise targeting system
  • Stay connected through advanced phone features
  • Navigate the city using transport networks
  • Immerse yourself in a digital world with emails, dating, and virtual activities
  • Challenge others in various multiplayer modes
