Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3

Challenge up to four friends in online competitions over a LAN or the Internet. Take them on in both Trick Attack and Graffiti modes.

The third installment in the legendary skateboarding series introduces revert mechanics, allowing for longer combo chains and more complex trick sequences. With enhanced graphics and new gameplay features, it set the standard for extreme sports games.

Key Features

  • Chain endless combos with new revert system
  • Challenge friends in intense four-player matches
  • Experience the ultimate skateboarding evolution with groundbreaking combo mechanics
  • Perfect advanced manuals and lip trick combos
  • Choose from iconic professional skater roster
  • Design unique skaters with custom appearance
  • Conquer diverse parks with special objectives
  • Shred to legendary punk and hip-hop tracks
  • Discover an extensive career mode with unique challenges and skill progression
  • Compete in thrilling Trick Attack and Graffiti
