Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3

Challenge up to four friends in online competitions over a LAN or the Internet. Take them on in both Trick Attack and Graffiti modes.

The third installment in the legendary skateboarding series introduces revert mechanics, allowing for longer combo chains and more complex trick sequences. With enhanced graphics and new gameplay features, it set the standard for extreme sports games.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary revert system for extended combos
  • Online multiplayer for up to 4 players
  • Trick Attack and Graffiti competitive game modes
  • Enhanced graphics engine with improved character models
  • Expanded roster of professional skateboarders
  • Create-a-Skater mode with extensive customization
  • Multiple skateparks with unique challenges and objectives
  • Soundtrack featuring punk, hip-hop, and alternative
  • Advanced trick system with manual and lip trick combinations
  • Career mode with progressive skill development
