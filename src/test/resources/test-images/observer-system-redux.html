Observer: System Redux

Observer: System Redux is a cyberpunk horror game set in a dystopian future Poland in the year 2084, where you play as Daniel Lazarski, an elite neural detective known as an Observer.

In this dark cyberpunk world ravaged by war, plague, and drugs, you can hack into the minds of suspects using a device called the Dream Eater. As you navigate through the twisted nightmares of your suspects' subconsciousness, you'll uncover their darkest fears and memories while investigating a complex case that will challenge your perception of reality.

Key Features

  • Hack suspects' minds to uncover their dark secrets
  • Explore dystopian cyberpunk world of future Poland
  • Experience mind-bending psychological horror in twisted digital nightmares
  • Investigate crime scenes using neural technology
  • Shape the investigation through crucial decisions
  • Navigate through mind-warping dream sequences
  • Immerse in atmospheric horror sound design
  • Uncover truth through detailed environments
  • Delve into a dark future where technology corrupts the minds and edge between human and machine distorts
  • Experience stunning visuals with ray-traced effects
