Observer: System Redux

Observer: System Redux is a cyberpunk horror game set in a dystopian future Poland in the year 2084, where you play as Daniel Lazarski, an elite neural detective known as an Observer.

In this dark cyberpunk world ravaged by war, plague, and drugs, you can hack into the minds of suspects using a device called the Dream Eater. As you navigate through the twisted nightmares of your suspects' subconsciousness, you'll uncover their darkest fears and memories while investigating a complex case that will challenge your perception of reality.

Key Features

  • Mind-hacking gameplay allowing exploration of suspects' fears and memories
  • Atmospheric cyberpunk dystopian setting
  • Psychological horror elements with reality-bending sequences
  • Enhanced visuals with ray-tracing and HDR
  • Detective gameplay with environmental investigation
  • Branching dialogue system affecting story progression
  • Immersive sound design creating a sense of dread and tension
  • Rutger Hauer's performance as protagonist
  • Multiple endings based on player choices and discoveries
