Red Dead Redemption

A Western epic set at the turn of the 20th century, following former outlaw John Marston as he hunts down the members of his old gang to secure his family's future.

Experience the twilight of the Wild West as John Marston, a former outlaw forced to hunt his old gang members. This open-world Western masterpiece combines compelling storytelling with authentic frontier atmosphere.

Key Features

  • Experience authentic Wild West at 20th century
  • Make your legend through the dynamic honor system
  • Explore a vast frontier from scorching deserts to snow-capped peaks and bustling towns
  • Master horseback riding and advanced bonding
  • Engage in thrilling gunfights with Dead Eye system
  • Survive dynamic weather affecting your journey
  • Challenge locals in classic Western frontier games
  • Uncover a powerful tale of redemption and justice
  • Experience the definitive Western adventure with authentic period detail and atmosphere
  • Face undead horrors in the nightmare expansion
