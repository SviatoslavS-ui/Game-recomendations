Metroid Prime

Samus returns in a new mission to unravel the mystery behind the ruined walls scattered across Tallon IV in this first-person adventure.

The first 3D Metroid game successfully translates the series' exploration-based gameplay into a first-person perspective. Players scan environments, solve puzzles, and battle Space Pirates while uncovering the secrets of the ancient Chozo civilization.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary first-person perspective for the Metroid series
  • Advanced scanning system for environmental storytelling
  • Multiple visors: Combat, Scan, Thermal, and X-Ray
  • Beam weapons: Power, Wave, Ice, and Plasma beams
  • Morph Ball mechanics in full 3D environment
  • Interconnected world design encouraging exploration
  • Lock-on targeting system for precise combat
  • Atmospheric soundtrack enhancing immersion
  • Environmental puzzles requiring different suit upgrades
  • Epic boss battles against massive creatures
