StarCraft 2

Return to the Koprulu Sector where three distinct species—Terran, Protoss, and Zerg—battle for dominance among the stars. Continue the epic saga in this award-winning real-time strategy game that redefined the genre for a new generation.

With its perfectly balanced gameplay and compelling narrative spanning three campaigns, StarCraft 2 offers unparalleled strategic depth and replayability. Command massive armies, research powerful technologies, and master the unique abilities of each race to claim victory in the ultimate sci-fi strategy experience.

Key Features

  • Three distinct playable races with unique units
  • Epic campaign spanning Wings of Liberty, Heart of the Swarm, and Legacy of the Void
  • Competitive multiplayer with advanced matchmaking
  • Robust map editor allowing for custom scenarios
  • Co-op missions featuring unique commanders with special abilities
  • Regular balance updates and seasonal content
  • Esports integration with professional tournaments
  • Advanced unit control and macro management
  • Cinematic storytelling with memorable characters
  • Extensive achievement system and progression rewards
