Halo: Combat Evolved

In a desperate attempt to keep the Covenant from finding Earth, you must uncover Halo's horrible secret and destroy mankind's nemesis.

The game that revolutionized console first-person shooters, introducing Master Chief and the expansive Halo universe. Experience epic battles against the alien Covenant while uncovering the mysteries of the ancient Halo ringworld.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary console FPS controls
  • Iconic weapons including the Assault Rifle and Energy Sword
  • Vehicle combat with Warthogs, Ghosts, and Banshees
  • Regenerating shield system changing combat dynamics
  • Epic sci-fi storyline spanning the mysterious Halo ringworld
  • Diverse alien enemies with unique behaviors
  • Memorable soundtrack enhancing the epic atmosphere
  • Large-scale battles with allied Marines
  • Split-screen multiplayer with various game modes
  • Seamless indoor and outdoor environments
