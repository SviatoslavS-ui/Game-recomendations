Halo: Combat Evolved

In a desperate attempt to keep the Covenant from finding Earth, you must uncover Halo's horrible secret and destroy mankind's nemesis.

The game that revolutionized console first-person shooters, introducing Master Chief and the expansive Halo universe. Experience epic battles against the alien Covenant while uncovering the mysteries of the ancient Halo ringworld.

Key Features

  • Master revolutionary console shooter mechanics
  • Wield iconic weapons from human and alien arsenals
  • Command an arsenal of vehicles from Warthogs to alien ships across vast battlefields
  • Deploy tactical shield system during combat
  • Uncover mysteries of the ancient Halo ringworld
  • Battle diverse alien forces across the ring
  • Lead Marines through large-scale conflicts
  • Challenge friends in split-screen combat modes
  • Experience seamless combat from tight corridors to vast outdoor environments
  • Immerse yourself in legendary sci-fi soundtrack
