Fallout 3

Set in the post-apocalyptic ruins of Washington D.C. in the year 2277, Fallout 3 revolutionized the series by transitioning from isometric turn-based gameplay to an immersive first/third-person perspective with real-time combat. You play as the Lone Wanderer, who emerges from the shelter of Vault 101 in search of their father, only to become entangled in a struggle that will determine the fate of the Capital Wasteland.

In this retro-futuristic world devastated by nuclear war, players must navigate the dangerous ruins of the capital, interact with various factions, and make moral choices that will impact the wasteland's future. The game seamlessly blends elements of action, RPG, and survival genres while maintaining the series' signature dark humor and retrofuturistic aesthetic.

Key Features

  • V.A.T.S. combat system for precision shot targeting
  • Explore the ruins of Washington D.C. in open world
  • Revolutionary S.P.E.C.I.A.L. system lets you build unique characters with perks and traits for endless builds
  • Shape the wasteland through impactful moral choices
  • Customize weapons with various modification options
  • Master many unique skills to evolve your character
  • Switch seamlessly between first and third person views
  • Engage in branching dialogues with multiple outcomes and consequences across the Capital Wasteland
  • Manage your journey with the Pip-Boy 3000 interface
  • Experience a retro-future world with atomic charm
