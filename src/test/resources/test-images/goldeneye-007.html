GoldenEye 007

You are Bond. James Bond. You are assigned covert operations connected with the GoldenEye weapons satellite.

The legendary Nintendo 64 shooter that proved first-person shooters could work on consoles. Based on the 1995 James Bond film, it features both an engaging single-player campaign and revolutionary split-screen multiplayer.

Key Features

  • Groundbreaking console FPS gameplay and controls
  • Iconic split-screen multiplayer for up to 4 players
  • Diverse mission objectives beyond simple shooting
  • Multiple difficulty levels affecting mission complexity
  • Authentic James Bond weapons and gadgets
  • Varied environments from military bases to tropical locations
  • Stealth gameplay elements and alarm systems
  • Memorable characters from the Bond universe
  • Unlockable cheats and bonus content
  • Revolutionary analog stick aiming for precise shooting
