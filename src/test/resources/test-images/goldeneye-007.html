GoldenEye 007

You are Bond. James Bond. You are assigned covert operations connected with the GoldenEye weapons satellite.

The legendary Nintendo 64 shooter that proved first-person shooters could work on consoles. Based on the 1995 James Bond film, it features both an engaging single-player campaign and revolutionary split-screen multiplayer.

Key Features

  • Experience groundbreaking console FPS mechanics
  • Compete in split-screen matches with up to 4 agents
  • Complete diverse missions with innovative objectives and multiple approach options
  • Master challenging missions across difficulty modes
  • Utilize authentic Bond weapons and spy gadgets
  • Infiltrate varied locations as Agent 007
  • Use stealth tactics to complete your missions
  • Meet legendary characters from Bond universe
  • Explore diverse environments from secret facilities to exotic locations with unique objectives
  • Perfect your aim with revolutionary control system
