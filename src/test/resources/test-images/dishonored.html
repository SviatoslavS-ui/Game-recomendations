Dishonored

Dishonored is an immersive first-person action game that casts you as a supernatural assassin driven by revenge. Framed for the murder of the Empress you swore to protect, you become an infamous assassin in a plague-ridden industrial city.

The truth behind your betrayal is as murky as the waters flooding the streets. In a time of uncertainty, when the city is besieged by plague and ruled by a corrupt government armed with industrial technologies, dark forces conspire to bestow upon you abilities beyond those of any common man.

Key Features

  • Supernatural assassin abilities like teleportation
  • Flexible combat system allowing lethal or non-lethal approaches with moral consequences related to your choices
  • Steampunk-inspired world with unique art direction
  • Branching interactive narrative with multiple ends
  • Innovative stealth mechanics with vertical exploration and creative problem solving
  • Intricately designed levels with multiple paths
  • Distinctive characters and immersive storytelling
  • Unique gadgets and weapons for creative gameplay
  • Dynamic AI that responds to your actions
  • Atmospheric immersive world building with rich environmental storytelling
