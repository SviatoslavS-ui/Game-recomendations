Dying Light

In the quarantined city of Harran, you play as Kyle Crane, an undercover operative sent to infiltrate the area following a mysterious viral outbreak. By day, you scavenge for supplies in this vast urban environment overrun by the infected. By night, the hunters become the hunted as the infected transform into something far more dangerous.

Combining first-person action, parkour movement, and survival horror elements, Dying Light challenges you to use your wits, agility, and improvised weapons to survive in a city where every decision could be your last. As you navigate the moral complexities of your mission, you must decide whether to follow orders or help the remaining survivors.

Key Features

  • Fluid parkour mechanics across urban environments
  • Morally complex narrative where your choices impact the fate of Harran's survivors and reveal the dark truth behind the viral outbreak
  • Day-night cycle altering zombie behavior patterns
  • First-person melee combat with weapon degradation
  • Extensive crafting system with over 100 blueprints
  • Open world with distinct districts to scavenge
  • Character progression through three skill trees
  • UV light tactics against nighttime predators
  • Environmental traps to outsmart the infected
  • Dynamic co-op multiplayer allowing friends to join your struggle against the infected hordes, complete with competitive challenges that test your survival skills
