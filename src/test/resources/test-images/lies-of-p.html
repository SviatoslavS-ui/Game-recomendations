Lies of P

In a dark Belle Époque world, the once beautiful city of Krat has fallen to madness and bloodshed. Playing as P, a puppet mechanoid, you must find your creator Geppetto and unravel the mysteries of this fallen city.

This soulslike action RPG reimagines the classic tale of Pinocchio in a brutal, twisted Belle Époque setting. As you fight through the streets of Krat, your choices will shape your journey and determine your path to humanity, where lying might be the only way to survive in a world filled with unspeakable horrors.

Key Features

  • Master precise combat with mechanical weaponry
  • Shape your destiny through choices between lies
  • Experience a dark twisted world where mechanical horrors roam Belle Époque streets
  • Customize mechanical arm with combat abilities
  • Face challenging mechanical bosses in brutal combat
  • Evolve your puppet through advanced Legion system
  • Transform weapons through mechanical modifications
  • Develop humanity through your impactful choices
  • Craft and upgrade an extensive arsenal of weapons with intricate mechanical parts
  • Explore dark reimagining of Pinocchio's tale
