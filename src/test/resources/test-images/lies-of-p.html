Lies of P

In a dark Belle Époque world, the once beautiful city of Krat has fallen to madness and bloodshed. Playing as P, a puppet mechanoid, you must find your creator Geppetto and unravel the mysteries of this fallen city.

This soulslike action RPG reimagines the classic tale of Pinocchio in a brutal, twisted Belle Époque setting. As you fight through the streets of Krat, your choices will shape your journey and determine your path to humanity, where lying might be the only way to survive in a world filled with unspeakable horrors.

Key Features

  • Precision combat system with unique weapon assembly mechanics
  • Narrative choice system where lying affects story progression
  • Customizable mechanical arm with various combat
  • Challenging boss encounters with intricate attacks
  • Interconnected world with Gothic Belle Époque aesthetics environment
  • Legion system for upgrading and evolving puppet
  • Diverse weapon types with transformative combat
  • Humanity system affecting character development
  • Crafting and upgrade systems for weapons and equipment
  • Atmospheric rich storytelling inspired by the tale of Pinocchio
