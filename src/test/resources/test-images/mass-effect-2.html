Mass Effect 2

In this dark second chapter, humans are now faced with an even greater peril in this science fiction adventure set in a vast universe.

Commander Shepard returns in this epic space opera RPG, assembling a diverse team for a suicide mission. Experience refined combat, deeper character development, and moral choices that shape the galaxy's fate.

Key Features

  • Master tactical combat with devastating biotics
  • Forge deep bonds through character loyalty missions
  • Experience an epic narrative where your choices shape the fate of entire civilizations
  • Command elite squads in strategic combat missions
  • Explore diverse worlds across the vast galaxy
  • Upgrade Normandy with resources from missions
  • Shape your story through dialogue wheel choices
  • Build meaningful relationships with crew members
  • Your past choices cascade through the story creating unique paths and consequences
  • Lead your team through the ultimate suicide mission
