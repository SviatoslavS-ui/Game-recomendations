Age of Wonders 4

Rule a fantasy realm of your own design in Age of Wonders 4, a blend of strategy, role-playing, and turn-based combat. Powerful Wizard Kings have returned to the realms to reign as gods among mortals, and you will rise to challenge their rule.

Unleash arcane destruction using Tomes of Magic, evolve your armies, and gain new abilities and forms. For the first time in the series, create your own custom factions, and leave your mark on countless worlds through each choice you make.

Key Features

  • Design unique faction with custom traits and powers
  • Master deep tactical combat with strategic depth
  • Experience branching stories with dynamic outcome
  • Explore procedurally generated realms filled with diverse biomes and valuable magical resources
  • Transform armies through mystical enhancements
  • Lead armies across varied landscapes and terrain
  • Build alliances or wage wars through diplomacy
  • Research ancient tomes of magic to unlock powerful spells and transform your races into new forms
  • Forge your units in combat veterans and champions
  • Compete or cooperate in epic multiplayer campaigns
