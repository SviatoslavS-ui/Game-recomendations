Age of Wonders 4

Rule a fantasy realm of your own design in Age of Wonders 4, a blend of strategy, role-playing, and turn-based combat. Powerful Wizard Kings have returned to the realms to reign as gods among mortals, and you will rise to challenge their rule.

Unleash arcane destruction using Tomes of Magic, evolve your armies, and gain new abilities and forms. For the first time in the series, create your own custom factions, and leave your mark on countless worlds through each choice you make.

Key Features

  • Create factions with unique forms and traits
  • Deep turn-based tactical combat with strategy depth
  • Dynamic event system providing rich storytelling
  • Evolve your units through magical transformation
  • Explore procedurally generated realms with varied biomes, obstacles and resources
  • Research powerful tomes of magic with unique spells tree and race modifiers
  • Engage in diplomacy or wage war against rival empire
  • Multiplayer support for competitive or cooperative
