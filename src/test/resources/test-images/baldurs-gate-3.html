Baldur's Gate 3

An ancient evil has returned to Baldur's Gate. Gather your party and return to the Forgotten Realms in a tale of fellowship and betrayal.

This epic RPG brings the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition ruleset to life with unprecedented freedom and depth. Create your character, form relationships, and make choices that shape your unique story in the Forgotten Realms.

Key Features

  • Full D&D 5th Edition rules implementation
  • Turn-based tactical combat with environmental interactions
  • Character creation with 12 classes and 46 subclasses
  • Meaningful choices affecting story and character relationships
  • 4-player cooperative multiplayer campaign
  • Romance options with complex companion relationships
  • Over 600 spells and abilities from D&D
  • Multiple solutions to quests and encounters
  • Rich dialogue system with extensive voice acting
  • Massive world with multiple acts and locations
