Baldur's Gate 3

An ancient evil has returned to Baldur's Gate. Gather your party and return to the Forgotten Realms in a tale of fellowship and betrayal.

This epic RPG brings the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition ruleset to life with unprecedented freedom and depth. Create your character, form relationships, and make choices that shape your unique story in the Forgotten Realms.

Key Features

  • Experience authentic D&D Fifth Edition in action
  • Strategic combat with environmental interactions
  • Build meaningful relationships through dialogue paths,decisions and choices
  • Deep dialogue system featuring extensive voice acting and evolving relationships with companions
  • Embark on adventures with four-player party system
  • Discover and master hundreds of spells from D&D
  • Tactical combat system integrating environmental hazards and magical synergies in battle
  • Shape your adventure through meaningful decisions
  • Explore diverse realms across an epic adventure
  • Create powerful alliances in living fantasy world
