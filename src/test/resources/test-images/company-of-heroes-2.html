Company of Heroes 2

Experience the brutal Eastern Front of World War II through the eyes of the Soviet Red Army. Command your forces through the harsh winter as you repel the German invasion in this critically acclaimed real-time strategy game.

With its revolutionary True Sight system and dynamic weather conditions, Company of Heroes 2 brings unprecedented realism to the battlefield. Lead your troops through snow-covered landscapes where strategic decisions and tactical prowess determine victory in the most unforgiving theater of war.

Key Features

  • Experience dynamic combat with True Sight mechanics
  • Revolutionary weather mechanics affecting unit movement, survival and combat abilities
  • Master tactical combat with cover and unit flanking
  • Command diverse units with faction-based abilities
  • Experience intense Eastern Front campaign battles
  • Engage in strategic multiplayer combat operations
  • Theater of War missions offering focused historical scenarios based on real events and battlefields
  • Deploy advanced squad tactics in realistic combat
  • Unlock powerful commander abilities and strategies
  • Fight through authentic World War II battlefields
