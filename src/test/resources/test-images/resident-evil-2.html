Resident Evil 2

In the dystopian metropolis of Raccoon City, rookie police officer Leon S. Kennedy and college student Claire Redfield are thrust together by a disastrous outbreak that transformed the city's population into deadly zombies.

This survival horror masterpiece from Capcom reimagines the 1998 classic with stunning visuals and modernized gameplay. Navigate through the zombie-infested Raccoon City Police Department and beyond, as you uncover the sinister conspiracy behind the outbreak and fight to survive the nightmare.

Key Features

  • Photorealistic visuals powered by the RE Engine
  • Intense over-the-shoulder third-person combat
  • Two unique campaigns with different characters
  • Horrifying zombies with realistic damage modeling
  • Atmospheric lighting with flashlight mechanics
  • Strategic inventory management system requiring careful planning of resources and weapons to survive the horror
  • Terrifying encounters with the unstoppable Tyrant
  • Intricate puzzle-solving throughout Raccoon City
  • Interwoven narrative paths that change based on character choice, leading to distinct story outcomes
  • Faithful reimagining of a survival horror classic
