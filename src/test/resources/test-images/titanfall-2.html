Titanfall 2

Experience the ultimate combination of speed and power in Titanfall 2, where agile Pilots and powerful Titans battle side by side on the frontiers of space. Master an advanced movement system that lets you slide, wall-run, and double-jump through intense, cinematic battlefields.

The single-player campaign follows the bond between Pilot Jack Cooper and BT-7274, a Vanguard-class Titan, as they fight against overwhelming odds to complete a mission they were never meant to carry out. In multiplayer, customize your Pilot and Titan to match your playstyle and dominate the battlefield with unique abilities and weapons.

Key Features

  • Fluid, fast-paced movement system with parkour-style wall-running and sliding
  • Pilot-Titan gameplay with seamless transitions
  • Compelling single-player campaign with innovative level design
  • Diverse multiplayer modes for competitive play
  • Six unique Titan classes with distinct weapons
  • Extensive Pilot and Titan customization options
  • Network system for easy matchmaking with friends
  • Dynamic maps featuring environmental hazards and interactions with Titans
  • Progressive unlocking system for weapons and equipment for both Pilots and Titans
  • Regular free content updates including maps & modes
