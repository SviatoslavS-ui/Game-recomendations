Total War: Warhammer III

The cataclysmic conclusion to the Total War: Warhammer trilogy. Rally your forces and step into the Realm of Chaos, a dimension of mind-bending horror where the fate of the world will be decided.

This grand strategy game combines turn-based empire building with massive real-time battles as you command one of seven unique races in a campaign to either save or dominate the Warhammer Fantasy world. Lead your armies, manage your realm, and forge your legend in the ultimate fantasy strategy experience.

Key Features

  • Command seven unique races with powerful armies
  • Lead epic expeditions into the corrupted realms
  • Experience epic fantasy warfare where thousands of troops clash in massive tactical battles
  • Forge powerful alliances through deep diplomacy
  • Choose legendary lords with powerful unique skills
  • Build mighty armies from vast unit collections
  • Conquer cities through massive siege battles
  • Join epic campaigns with up to eight players
  • Discover a dynamic campaign where your choices shape the fate of all realms
  • Unite three massive maps in Immortal Empires
