Total War: Warhammer III

The cataclysmic conclusion to the Total War: Warhammer trilogy. Rally your forces and step into the Realm of Chaos, a dimension of mind-bending horror where the fate of the world will be decided.

This grand strategy game combines turn-based empire building with massive real-time battles as you command one of seven unique races in a campaign to either save or dominate the Warhammer Fantasy world. Lead your armies, manage your realm, and forge your legend in the ultimate fantasy strategy experience.

Key Features

  • Seven unique playable races with distinct mechanics and units
  • Realm of Chaos campaign with expeditions into the daemon realms
  • Massive real-time battles with thousands units
  • Deep diplomatic systems with alliances and vassals
  • Legendary lords with personal quest chains and abilities
  • Customizable armies with extensive unit rosters
  • Siege rework with expanded settlement battles
  • Multiplayer campaigns with up to eight players
  • Dynamic narrative events affecting campaign progression
  • Immortal Empires mode combining all three maps
