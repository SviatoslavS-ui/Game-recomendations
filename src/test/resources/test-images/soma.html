SOMA

SOMA is a sci-fi horror game from Frictional Games, the creators of Amnesia: The Dark Descent. It is set beneath the ocean's surface at the remote underwater facility PATHOS-II.

The game explores profound questions about consciousness, identity, and what it means to be human in a world where consciousness can be digitized. As machines start to take on human traits and humans become more machine-like, the line between human and machine begins to blur.

Key Features

  • Immersive underwater setting with haunting environments
  • Philosophical narrative exploring consciousness and identity
  • Survival horror gameplay with stealth mechanics
  • Atmospheric sound design enhancing the sense of isolation
  • Interactive storytelling with meaningful choices
  • Unique monster designs and behaviors
  • Puzzle-solving elements integrated into the narrative
  • No combat system, focusing on avoidance and stealth
  • Multiple endings based on player decisions
  • Safe Mode option for players who want to experience the story without monster threats
