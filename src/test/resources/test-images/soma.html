SOMA

SOMA is a sci-fi horror game from Frictional Games, the creators of Amnesia: The Dark Descent. It is set beneath the ocean's surface at the remote underwater facility PATHOS-II.

The game explores profound questions about consciousness, identity, and what it means to be human in a world where consciousness can be digitized. As machines start to take on human traits and humans become more machine-like, the line between human and machine begins to blur.

Key Features

  • Explore haunting underwater facilities in the abyss
  • Question what remains of humanity in digital minds
  • Immerse yourself in a philosophical journey exploring consciousness and human identity
  • Survive hostile entities through stealth mechanics
  • Shape your fate through meaningful story choices
  • Uncover dark truths through intricate puzzles
  • Experience crushing isolation in ocean depths
  • Navigate through mysterious underwater ruins
  • Discover a groundbreaking narrative that challenges your perception of consciousness
  • Choose between standard or story-focused modes
