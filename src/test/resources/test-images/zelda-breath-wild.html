The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Step into a world of discovery, exploration and adventure in this stunning open-air adventure across the ruined kingdom of Hyrule.

This revolutionary open-world Zelda game encourages nonlinear gameplay with a consistent physics engine that lets players approach problems in multiple ways. The chemistry engine governs how objects interact, creating an unstructured world that rewards experimentation.

Key Features

  • Explore vast untamed wild areas of ancient Hyrule
  • Master innovative physics to solve unique puzzles
  • Experience boundless adventure in a breathtaking world shaped by living physics
  • Discover countless ways to overcome challenges
  • Create powerful elixirs through magical alchemy
  • Wield ancient Sheikah technology with mastery
  • Conquer challenging shrines and divine beasts
  • Uncover sacred secrets throughout the realm
  • Journey through a dynamic world where weather and nature shape your epic adventure
  • Chart your own legendary path to save Hyrule
