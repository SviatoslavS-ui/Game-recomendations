The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Step into a world of discovery, exploration and adventure in this stunning open-air adventure across the ruined kingdom of Hyrule.

This revolutionary open-world Zelda game encourages nonlinear gameplay with a consistent physics engine that lets players approach problems in multiple ways. The chemistry engine governs how objects interact, creating an unstructured world that rewards experimentation.

Key Features

  • Massive open-world Hyrule with complete freedom of exploration
  • Revolutionary physics and chemistry engine
  • Climbing, swimming, and paragliding with stamina-based mechanics
  • Weapon degradation system encouraging tactical resource management
  • Complex cooking system for meals and elixirs
  • Sheikah Slate with remote bombs and magnesis
  • 120 shrine puzzles and Divine Beast mechanical dungeons
  • 900 Korok Seeds for inventory expansion challenges
  • Dynamic weather system affecting gameplay and combat
  • Nonlinear story progression
