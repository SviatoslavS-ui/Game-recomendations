Far Cry 5

Welcome to Hope County, Montana, a picturesque region that has been seized by a fanatical doomsday cult called the Project at Eden's Gate. As a junior deputy sheriff, you become trapped in this isolated community and must work with the resistance to liberate the county from the grip of Joseph Seed and his siblings.

Experience an open world filled with danger and opportunity where you can go guns blazing or use stealth to take down the cult. Recruit allies, tame wild animals, and commandeer vehicles to build your resistance against Eden's Gate in this thrilling first-person shooter adventure.

Key Features

  • Explore Montana's rugged terrain filled with secrets
  • Recruit unique companions with specialized abilities
  • Pilot diverse vehicles from muscle cars to aircraft
  • Customize weapons with scavenged materials and parts
  • Disrupt cult operations through strategic sabotage
  • Immerse yourself in a living ecosystem where your actions impact the resistance movement against Eden's Gate, altering how NPCs respond to your presence throughout Hope County
  • Experience a dynamic narrative that branches based on your decisions, leading to multiple possible conclusions to the conflict against the doomsday cult
  • Craft weapons and resources from materials found
  • Interact with wildlife as both threat and resource
  • Adapt combat tactics to overcome diverse challenges
