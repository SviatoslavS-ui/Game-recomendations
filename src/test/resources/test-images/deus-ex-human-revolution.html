Deus Ex: Human Revolution

In the near future, humanity stands divided. Powerful corporations enhance humans with mechanical augmentations, but those who can afford these enhancements are the privileged few, creating a new social divide.

As Adam Jensen, a security specialist with cutting-edge augmentations, you'll unravel a global conspiracy in this cyberpunk action RPG where your choices determine the outcome. Navigate a world of intrigue, where every decision shapes both the story and your character's development.

Key Features

  • Complete missions through stealth, combat, or talks
  • Enhance your abilities with cybernetic augmentations
  • Shape the story through meaningful dialogue choices
  • Explore a detailed cyberpunk world where advanced augmentations divide society and shape humanity
  • Master intricate hacking to bypass digital defenses
  • Engage in dialogue with advanced persuasion options
  • Customize weapons with powerful tech modifications
  • Experience rich atmosphere of dystopian future
  • Navigate through a complex web of conspiracies where every choice impacts global events
  • Discover dark secrets behind human enhancement
