Grand Theft Auto III

Players are put at the heart of their very own gangster movie, and let loose in a fully-realised 3 dimensional city.

The groundbreaking entry that revolutionized open-world gaming, GTA III introduced players to Liberty City in full 3D. This game established the template for modern sandbox games with its freedom of exploration and non-linear gameplay.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary 3D open-world city environment
  • Complete freedom to explore Liberty City
  • Diverse vehicle types from cars to helicopters
  • Radio stations with licensed music and talk shows
  • Non-linear mission structure with multiple approaches
  • Wanted level system with escalating police response
  • Varied mission types from racing to assassination
  • Hidden packages and bonus content throughout the city
  • Realistic physics and vehicle handling
  • Immersive urban atmosphere with pedestrian AI
