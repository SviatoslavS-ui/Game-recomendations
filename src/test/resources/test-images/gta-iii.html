Grand Theft Auto III

Players are put at the heart of their very own gangster movie, and let loose in a fully-realised 3 dimensional city.

The groundbreaking entry that revolutionized open-world gaming, GTA III introduced players to Liberty City in full 3D. This game established the template for modern sandbox games with its freedom of exploration and non-linear gameplay.

Key Features

  • Experience revolutionary 3D open-world city design
  • Explore the vast criminal underworld of Liberty City
  • Master an extensive range of vehicles with realistic handling and unique driving mechanics
  • Enjoy iconic radio stations with unique content
  • Choose your path through branching crime missions
  • Face escalating police response during crimes
  • Take on diverse missions across urban landscape
  • Find hidden packages scattered through the city
  • Experience a living breathing city with dynamic AI systems and emergent gameplay
  • Complete missions through multiple approaches
