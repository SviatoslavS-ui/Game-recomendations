Super Mario Odyssey

Mario embarks on a new journey through unknown worlds in the first sandbox-style Mario game since Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Sunshine.

Join Mario and his new ally Cappy on a massive, globe-trotting 3D adventure. Use amazing new abilities to collect Power Moons and save Princess Peach from Bowser's wedding plans across diverse kingdoms.

Key Features

  • Master Cappy's possession powers to control foes
  • Explore vast kingdoms filled with hidden treasures
  • Experience an incredible journey through diverse worlds from bustling cities to ruins
  • Discover power moons across sandbox kingdoms
  • Capture perfect moments with photo mode system
  • Collect iconic outfits from Mario's adventures
  • Control Mario with precise motion mechanics
  • Unlock secret kingdoms in post-game content
  • Embark on an epic adventure where classic 2D and modern 3D gameplay perfectly merge
  • Complete challenging missions in magical realms
