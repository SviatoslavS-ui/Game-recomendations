Super Mario Odyssey

Mario embarks on a new journey through unknown worlds in the first sandbox-style Mario game since Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Sunshine.

Join Mario and his new ally Cappy on a massive, globe-trotting 3D adventure. Use amazing new abilities to collect Power Moons and save Princess Peach from Bowser's wedding plans across diverse kingdoms.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary "Capture" ability to control enemies and objects
  • Cappy companion providing new movement and combat options
  • Diverse kingdoms from urban New Donk City to prehistoric lands
  • Sandbox-style exploration with multiple objectives per area
  • Photo Mode for capturing memorable moments
  • Costume collection with outfits from Mario's history
  • Motion control integration for enhanced gameplay
  • Post-game content with additional kingdoms
  • Seamless transitions between 2D and 3D gameplay sections
