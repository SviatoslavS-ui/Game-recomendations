The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

A legendary adventure that redefined gaming with its innovative Z-targeting system and immersive 3D world.

Set in a fantasy action-adventure world with an expansive three-dimensional environment, this game introduced groundbreaking techniques that became industry standards. Players control Link from a third-person perspective as he embarks on an epic quest through time itself.

Key Features

  • Master innovative combat with precise Z-targeting
  • Perform intuitive actions with context controls
  • Experience an epic journey through time as both child and adult in mystical Hyrule
  • Play enchanted melodies on the mystical Ocarina
  • Explore vast dungeons filled with ancient puzzles
  • Wield legendary weapons and mysterious magic arts
  • Complete engaging quests across ancient lands
  • Battle mighty bosses in legendary encounters
  • Discover a timeless tale where destiny unfolds across two parallel dimensions
  • Journey through a revolutionary 3D adventure
