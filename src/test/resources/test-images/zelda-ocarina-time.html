The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

A legendary adventure that redefined gaming with its innovative Z-targeting system and immersive 3D world.

Set in a fantasy action-adventure world with an expansive three-dimensional environment, this game introduced groundbreaking techniques that became industry standards. Players control Link from a third-person perspective as he embarks on an epic quest through time itself.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary Z-targeting system for precise combat and interaction
  • Context-sensitive actions that simplify controls
  • Time travel mechanics, play both young and adult
  • Musical gameplay with the Ocarina of Time instrument
  • Expansive 3D world with real-time polygonal graphics
  • Complex dungeons with puzzles and boss battles
  • Side quests including trading sequences and riding
  • Multiple weapons including swords, bows, bombs, and magic spells
  • Stealth gameplay elements in certain sections
