Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2

The galaxy is in peril. Entire worlds are falling to the relentless onslaught of Tyranid hordes. As Lieutenant Titus of the Ultramarines, you stand as humanity's last hope against the overwhelming alien threat.

This action-packed third-person shooter continues the story of the iconic Space Marine, bringing the brutal combat of the Warhammer 40,000 universe to life. Wield devastating weapons, unleash powerful abilities, and experience the weight and might of an elite Adeptus Astartes warrior as you battle alongside your battle-brothers against the endless swarm.

Key Features

  • Visceral combat blending melee and ranged warfare seamlessly
  • Three-player cooperative campaign with distinct Space Marine classes
  • Massive battles against thousands of Tyranid enemies simultaneously
  • Customizable weapons and armor with chapter-specific upgrades
  • Cinematic storytelling in the rich and detailed Warhammer 40,000 universe
  • Devastating and advanced execution moves providing tactical advantages
  • Advanced movement system with jump packs
  • Detailed environments across war-torn Imperial worlds
  • Progression system with unlockable abilities
  • Competitive multiplayer modes with various Space Marine chapters
