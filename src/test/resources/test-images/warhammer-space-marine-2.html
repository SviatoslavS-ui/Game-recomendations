Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2

The galaxy is in peril. Entire worlds are falling to the relentless onslaught of Tyranid hordes. As Lieutenant Titus of the Ultramarines, you stand as humanity's last hope against the overwhelming alien threat.

This action-packed third-person shooter continues the story of the iconic Space Marine, bringing the brutal combat of the Warhammer 40,000 universe to life. Wield devastating weapons, unleash powerful abilities, and experience the weight and might of an elite Adeptus Astartes warrior as you battle alongside your battle-brothers against the endless swarm.

Key Features

  • Master brutal combat mixing melee and ranged power
  • Fight alongside battle-brothers in epic operations
  • Experience epic-scale warfare against endless hordes of nightmarish alien swarms
  • Customize weapons with devastating chapter upgrades
  • Immerse in rich Warhammer 40K universe and lore
  • Execute enemies with devastating crushing finishes
  • Soar through battles with enhanced jump packs
  • Battle across vast war-torn Imperial worlds
  • Discover a dark future where superhuman warriors defend mankind from alien horrors
  • Unlock powerful abilities through rank progression
